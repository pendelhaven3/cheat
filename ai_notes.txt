AI Level 1:
a. Will always play allowed cards if able.
b. If cannot play an allowed card value, will randomly select an available card then declare it as a random allowed card value.
c. Will only challenge based on current turn data.

e.g. If you declare 2 aces and they have 3 aces on their hand, then they will challenge.

AI Level 2:
a. Will always play allowed cards if able.
b. If cannot play an allowed card value, will randomly select an available card then declare it as a random allowed card value
   but taking note of earlier declared values during the round.
c. Will challenge based on current round data

e.g. If you declare 2 aces and a previous player has also declared 2 aces and they have 1 ace on their hand, then they will challenge.
